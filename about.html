Mfondo Logistics (Pty) Ltd

We provide a professional client focused logistics business which specializes in yellow plant and mining equipment. As a fast growing company, we go above and beyond on every service we provide to our clients and we deliver on our promises with integrity. Mfondo Logistics was found in 2020 by it’s founder and director Dr Jerry Zitha as a 100% black owned business, based in Mpumalanga province with operations in various provinces around South Africa. We offer a wide range of Yellow plant services as well as the Mining equipment and vehicle services. Our commitment to outstanding job completion, exceptional customer service and superior safety performance has made us a partner of choice in the logistics industry. 

OUR CLIENT

We are committed to the changing needs of our client and our business will evolve to ensure to continue to satisfy their requirements. We work with our clients in an open and collaborative way and have a desire to prove that we can provide the best services. We treat our clients business as our business by providing value for money and obtaining the most competitive pricing. We are committed to providing the highest quality service to our clients and to add value to their investment. Our end to end engineering processes and systems provide the means by which we can deliver our service and commitments. 

OUR TEAM

Over the years we have built a stable workforce with a large number of our employees being 'long term employees. Loyalty and commitment are values we promote among  all our employees, from trainees through to senior management 

OUR SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITIES

We are committed to building a sustainable future for our company and the staff we employ by continuing to work with and in our local community to achieve success in all we do. We support our local community by providing career opportunities for local people as our contribution to the community and our country.
